About me

I'm an entrepreneur, mobile developer, gadget enthusiast and I love to read. I've been interested in technology since I was very young, especially computers.

I graduated with a Bachelor degree in Computing Science in 2012. When smartphones got around, I really got hooked. In 2012 I co-founded Digitalisma, to provide customers with the best Mobile experience possible.

Apart from technology, I’m a sports fanatic: I play basketball from when I was 5, go to the gym 4 times per week on average, and have been coaching teams for 10+ years. I’m always on the lookout to learn new skills, and I love to use my time (and of others) as effectively as possible.

I excel at finding creative solutions to problems, which contributes to everything I think and do. I question everything, because if nothing is sure, everything is possible.

Life shouldn’t be hard when we have so much technology to help us.
