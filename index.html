About me

I'm an entrepreneur, mobile developer, gadget enthusiast and I love to read. I've been interested in technology since I was very young, especially computers.

I graduated with a Bachelor degree in Computing Science in 2012. When smartphones got around, I really got hooked. In 2012 I co-founded Digitalisma, to provide customers with the best Mobile experience possible.

I like to lead teams, so I can learn something from them and they from me. I'm always on the lookout to learn new skills, and I love to use my time (and of others) as effectively as possible. I excel at finding creative solutions to problems, which contributes to everything I think and do.
